About me

The person behind the code

AM

Hi — I'm Gulshan, a San Francisco-based frontend engineer with seven years of experience building products for startups, scale-ups, and Fortune 500 companies. I care deeply about the craft of interface development and the people who use the things we build.

My approach blends engineering rigour with a designer's eye. Before writing a single line of code, I ask why — why does this interaction exist, who benefits, and how can it be made more inclusive? That thinking shapes everything from component API design to animation timing.

Outside of work you'll find me teaching an accessibility workshop, contributing to open-source tooling, or photographing the edges of California's coast.
